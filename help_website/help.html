Creating an account

Upon booting up the mobile application, click the button "Signup", then follow the assign steps for creating an account.

Deleting Account and All Account Information

Navigate to the manage bottom tab, then the "settings" upper tab, then "delete account". This will delete the account and ALL associated information with the account. Note, this action cannot be undone under any circumstance.
